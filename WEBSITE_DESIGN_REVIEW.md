## 5. Design Improvement Recommendations

### A. Hero Section & First Impressions

**Current Issues (verified):**
- The tagline "Your SPOC for all your Solar needs" is decent but needs stronger visual impact
- Homepage must immediately establish trust — especially given the lack of online reviews
- First impression needs to overcome the credibility gap

**Recommendations:**
1. **Full-width hero video/image** — Use high-quality photos/video of real installation projects in Mumbai (rooftops with solar panels, the team at work, before/after shots). Avoid stock photos
2. **Bold headline** — e.g., *"Mumbai's Trusted Solar Partner — Power Your Home with Clean Energy"* or *"Save 70-90% on Electricity Bills with Rooftop Solar"*
3. **Sub-headline with USP** — *"End-to-End Solar Solutions | MNRE-Approved Tier-1 Equipment | 20+ Financing Partners"*
4. **Prominent CTA buttons** — Two buttons side by side:
   - "Get Free Site Assessment" (primary, bright color)
   - "Calculate Your Savings" (secondary, outline style)
5. **Trust badges** — Display equipment brand logos (Vikram Solar, Waaree, TATA, RenewSys), MNRE approval, and years of experience right below the hero
6. **Animated counter bar** — Show verifiable stats: "X Years Experience | X Installations | 70-90% Bill Reduction | 25-Year Warranty"
   - *Note: Use only real, verifiable numbers — inflated claims without evidence will backfire*

### C. Visual Design & Branding

**Recommendations:**

1. **Color Palette** — Adopt a modern, trust-inspiring color scheme:
   - **Primary:** Deep Solar Blue (#1A365D) — conveys trust and professionalism
   - **Secondary:** Vibrant Solar Orange/Amber (#F6A623) — energy, warmth, sun
   - **Accent:** Fresh Green (#38A169) — sustainability, eco-friendly
   - **Neutral:** Clean White (#FFFFFF) and Light Gray (#F7FAFC) — backgrounds
   - **Dark:** Charcoal (#2D3748) — text

2. **Typography:**
   - Headlines: Modern sans-serif (e.g., Poppins, Inter, or Montserrat) — Bold/Semi-Bold
   - Body text: Clean sans-serif (e.g., Open Sans, Inter) — Regular, 16px minimum
   - Maintain consistent hierarchy: H1 > H2 > H3 > Body

3. **Imagery:**
   - Use **real project photos** from Mumbai installations (not generic stock images)
   - Professional photography of your team at work
   - Before/after rooftop shots
   - Drone shots of completed installations
   - Customer photos (with permission) alongside testimonials

4. **Icons & Illustrations:**
   - Use a consistent icon set (e.g., Phosphor, Heroicons, or custom solar-themed icons)
   - Animated icons for services section
   - Infographic-style illustrations for "How Solar Works" section

5. **Whitespace & Layout:**
   - Generous padding between sections (80-120px)
   - Maximum content width of 1200px centered
   - Card-based layouts for services and products
   - Alternating light/dark section backgrounds for visual rhythm

6. **Branding Consistency:**
   - Align Instagram (@energyspoc) bio to focus on solar energy — remove or de-emphasize "Home Automation" reference unless it's a core offering
   - Use consistent logo, colors, and messaging across website, social media, and directory listings
